MANNING OPERATIONS

Centrofin Maritime Services Private Limited welcomes officers and Crew members across all ranks, to join us thereby availing various allied benefits along with an excellent on board package matching the best in the industry. We are committed to the seafarer and developing activities which promote the welfare there-off. In an ongoing endeavour to facilitate the same, Centrofin Maritime Services Private Limited has strategic tie-ups with leading shipping companies, operating various kinds of vessels worlds wide. These organizations are being provided comprehensive man power solutions by Centrofin Maritime Services Private Limited.

MARITIME CAREER CONSULTANCY

Merchant Navy offers a variety of avenues as in career choices. We as an organization ascertain the caliber of candidate and counsel the path accordingly. This apart our organization conducts career specific preparatory classes including soft skills development in order to ensure that the candidate concerned gets fully prepared to face the entrance examination conducted by the Academics and Shipping Companies, respectively. We provide in house counselling that imparts prudent information on various courses, we tied up with only Directorate General Shipping Government of India affiliated and reputed Pre Sea training centres only which provide quality training with good infrastructure facilities

ENTRY LEVEL SPONSORSHIP

Entry Level Sponsorship for Cadets, TMEs, Ratings, Retd. Naval Officers. We are pleased to inform that we are one of the very few organizations to provide a complete solution, offering a comprehensive guidance to Entry Level Candidates for joining Merchant Navy. We offer a fully sponsored program wherein the candidate joins a pre-sea Program followed by a sponsored on board cadetship with an attractive stipend. This program is also open to candidates who are willing to join Merchant Navy as Trainee Marine Engineers (Mechanical and Electrical Engineers).

SEAFARER'S LICENSE

Today, ship owners and managers face huge problems in meeting the STCW requirements regarding endorsements and certification of officers and crew. Centrofin Maritime Services Private Limited facilitates not only the requests of Ship Owners for the maritime industry, but also the seafarers. There are very few organizations that take into consideration the interest of the Seafarer and Centrofin Maritime Services Private Limited is one such organization.
